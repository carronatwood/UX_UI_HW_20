Hair Goals

This project sought out an opportunity to bring hair care products and knowledge to users who needed help understanding and caring for their hair. We wanted to remain accessible to all, but with a strong focus on textured hair. We identified the pain points of users through interviews and survey data and found that the mains struggles related to an abundance of misinformation and general lack of access to reliable information, along with this we found that people were finding it hard to access products in stores that would work well for their hair. To combat these issues, Hair Goals was born. We created the prototype for an app that would allow users to take a hair quiz to find out exactly what type and porosity they have, then make recommendations and provide a library of resources where they can do further reading about how to care for their unique type of hair, and an element of connection with other uses through messaging and the ability to write and like other posts. Along with this, we wanted to provide an optional subscription service that caters products directly to their individual needs and as a way to monetize the service while remaining accessible.



Project Phases

  • User Research
  • Definition
  • Ideation
  • Prototyping
  • User Testing
  • Conclusions




User Research



We started by defining our Proto Persona who helped us get a clear idea of exactly who we wanted to help with this app. Then we went on the Interviews and Survey portion of the reseearch which allowed us to refine our Proto Persona into Tabitha, our User Persona





Definition



After nailing down exactly who is in need of this product, we started looking at the data gathered in our research and created Affinity Diagrams and I Like, I Wish, What If's that allowed us to develop our user insight and problem statements





Ideation



Now, we have defined the problem we can start looking for solutions, we addressed the common themes - lack of access, lack of information, a feeling of know knowing what to trust - and started trying to solve these with our app - we wanted our focus to be information, access to the right products and a social platform to promote connection





Prototype



Our initial lo-fi sketched out wireframes were our first attempt to see how we wanted to address these needs, we wanted a simple and clean platform with easy access to the necessary pages - Profile, Library, Inspo, Shop, Messages. Through rounds of testing and iterations we found the most intutive and user friendly way to organize our screens and finally added high fidelity design and branding to create a beaultif and useful product to meet user needs





User Testing



Our user testing taught us that we had been issing a few essential thinges including back buttons in the splash screen/sign up flows. we also learned that many users loved and wanted to further expand the social aspect. We iterated and designed our high fidelity prototype with all of this feedback in mind





Conclusion



Upon finishing this project we felt that we has addressed the user needs and created an exciting new platform for a very under-served market, while remaing accessible to all hair types. We would love to further develop the social aspect for a more connected experience and find a way to "game-ify" with incentives and competitions in future iterations
